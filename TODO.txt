Version 0.10
------------
* Remove deprecated functions:
    - ``skimage.filter.rank.*``
* Remove deprecated parameter ``epsilon`` of ``skimage.viewer.LineProfile``
* Remove backwards-compatability of ``skimage.measure.regionprops``
* Remove deprecation of ``skimage.morphology.erosion`` and
  ``skimage.morphology.dilation`` and simply set as alias:
  ``erosion = skimage.filter.rank.minimum`` and
  ``dilation = skimage.filter.rank.maximum``
